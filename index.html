ABOUT ME

Ena Azevedo is a multidisciplinary artist based in Dubai with her husband and child. She is originally from Sarajevo. Her practice spans sculpture, painting, and special effects, with a focus on the human form and material exploration.

Her work developed from early recognition in painting, including multiple award-winning works and international exhibitions during her formative years. She later expanded her practice through studies in 3D design and artificial intelligence, bridging traditional artistic disciplines with contemporary techniques.

Azevedo’s artistic journey evolved from painting into sculptural and spatial work, leading to large-scale installations and experimental material-based pieces. Alongside her fine art practice, she works in film and collaborative projects across the GCC, creating prosthetics and masks that merge artistic expression with technical precision.

Her work continues to explore identity, form, and emotional presence through both physical and conceptual approaches.

01

Vision

To create work and to create beauty that transcends traditional boundaries between art, material, and human perception, exploring the relationship between form, identity, and emotional presence. Through sculptural and material-driven practices, the aim is to develop pieces that exist not only as objects, but as experiences within space.

02

Mission

To continuously inspire other artists to evolve a multidisciplinary practice that integrates experiment. To continue combining sculpture, painting, and mixed media, pushing the limits of material experimentation and artistic expression. Through both independent work and collaborative projects, the mission is to create meaningful, immersive pieces while mentoring and inspiring others to develop their own artistic voice.
