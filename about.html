ABOUT ME

Ena Azevedo

Ena Azevedo is a multidisciplinary artist based in Dubai with her husband and child. She is originally from Sarajevo. Her practice spans sculpture, painting, and special effects, with a focus on the human form and material exploration.

Her work developed from early recognition in painting, including multiple award-winning works and international exhibitions during her formative years. She later expanded her practice through studies in 3D design and artificial intelligence, bridging traditional artistic disciplines with contemporary techniques.

Azevedo’s artistic journey evolved from painting into sculptural and spatial work, leading to large-scale installations and experimental material-based pieces.

Alongside her fine art practice, she works in film and collaborative projects across the GCC, creating prosthetics and masks that merge artistic expression with technical precision.

Her work continues to explore identity, form, and emotional presence through both physical and conceptual approaches.
